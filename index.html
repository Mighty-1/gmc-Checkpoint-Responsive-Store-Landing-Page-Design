Sale &Purchase

With an already established and continuous presence in the S&P market over the past decade with numerous successful concluded deals in our record, our database includes vessels of all types and sizes including but not limited to ferries, tugs, offshore equipment, cargo vessels, tankers as well as small work and service boats. Consistent to our long standing experience, we provide turn-key, professional services starting from the initial negotiations of a sale/purchase of a vessel up to her delivery, covering the full spectrum of the S&P process.

Chartering

Our chartering department is actively involved and specialized in the dry and project cargo market. Through our worldwide shipping contacts (Charterers / Owners / Operators) as well as our exclusive agreements with commodity importers / exporters, we offer profitable opportunities to voyage / time or bareboat charter, providing thorough and competitive services adjusted to our versatile clientele's specific needs. Our experienced and capable team is always ready to provide custom cargoes for your vessel, whether a mini bulk or a caper.

Ship Management

Ship Management

Having in our records numerous Ship Delivery-Management projects and by providing Consultancy Services to our clients, our technical team now undertakes full management of vessels including among others issuance of the certification, class surveys, maintenance, crew for manning the vessel, bunkers/lubs, spares/stores/provisions, commercial use etc.

Our ship management team is a well specialized and experienced team, headed by Cpt Panos Makrygiannis who has 25 years of experience as Operational Manager at first class shipping companies together with our Chief Engineer Charis Makrygiannis.

Ship Deliveries

Steadfast to our policy throughout the years offering holistic and tailor-made services to our clients, our ship deliveries department undertakes vessel’s transportation from A to Z worldwide. Whether you are looking for an own power delivery or a towage, our expert Captains and Superintendent Engineers organize, arrange and manage the safe delivery of your vessel providing solid, complete and cost-effective solutions. Our services include and cover from necessary certification, crew, insurance, bunkering and technical vessel’s preparation up to day-to-day management and operations monitoring till her delivery to end destination.
